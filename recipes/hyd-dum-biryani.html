Hyderabad Dum Biryani

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Basmati Rice
  • 1 kg Chicken
  • 2 large Onions
  • 4-5 Green Chilies
  • 1 cup Yogurt
  • 2 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste
  • Whole Spices (Cinnamon, Cloves, Cardamom)
  • Fresh Mint and Coriander Leaves
  • Saffron (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil or Ghee
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes.
  2. Marinate the chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, green chilies, and spices for at least 1 hour.
  3. Fry the onions until golden brown and set aside.
  4. In a large pot, heat oil or ghee and add the marinated chicken. Cook until the chicken is tender.
  5. Add the soaked rice to the pot along with water and salt. Cook until the rice is 70% done.
  6. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken, sprinkle fried onions, mint, coriander, and saffron (if using).
  7. Cover the pot with a tight lid and cook on low heat (dum) for about 30-40 minutes.
  8. Serve hot with raita or salad.
  9. Enjoy your delicious Hyderabad Dum Biryani!
