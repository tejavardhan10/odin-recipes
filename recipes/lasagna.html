Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups marinara sauce
  • 1 lb ground beef or sausage
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil (optional)
  • Olive oil
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté garlic. Add ground beef or sausage and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, egg, salt, pepper, and half of the Parmesan cheese.
  5. Spread a layer of marinara sauce on the bottom of a baking dish.
  6. Layer 4 noodles, half of the ricotta mixture, half of the meat mixture, and a third of the mozzarella cheese.
  7. Repeat layers, finishing with noodles, marinara sauce, and remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese on top.
  8. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes until cheese is bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for 10 minutes before serving. Garnish with fresh basil if desired.
  10. Enjoy your delicious lasagna!
