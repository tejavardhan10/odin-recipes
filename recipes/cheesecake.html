Cheese Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 4 (8 oz) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • Fresh fruit or chocolate sauce for topping (optional)
  • Water

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325°F (160°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and sugar. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. In a large mixing bowl, beat cream cheese until smooth. Gradually add sugar and vanilla extract, mixing well.
  4. Add eggs one at a time, mixing on low speed after each addition. Do not overmix.
  5. Add sour cream and flour, mixing until just combined.
  6. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the crust in the springform pan.
  7. Bake for 50-60 minutes or until the center is set but still slightly jiggly.
  8. Turn off the oven and leave the cheesecake inside for 1 hour to cool gradually.
  9. Remove from oven and let it cool completely at room temperature. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours or overnight before serving.
  10. Top with fresh fruit or chocolate sauce if desired.
  11. Enjoy your delicious cheesecake!
